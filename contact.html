About Us

Welcome to Directa! Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and career by providing students with the resources they need to succeed. We offer:

  • Career Guidance: Internships, jobs, abroad opportunities, and government vacancies.
  • Projects & Research: Useful tools, software, and applications to help students excel.
  • Abroad Path: Real-life experiences shared by students and professionals working/studying abroad through YouTube and LinkedIn videos.

Our goal is to empower students with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed decisions about their future.

Privacy Policy

At Directa, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our website:

  • Data Collection: We do not collect or store any personal data from our users.
